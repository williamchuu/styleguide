COLOR

We aim for vibrant and bright color palettes for a fun, fresh, engaging, and dynamic orange feel. The harmony in app interface design is what we always aim for. In order to make the user feel satisfied and comfortable, we strive to bring a balance into the user interface design that is most attractive and effective for the user experience. Harmonized colors contribute to a good first impression from our app.

Visual colors are designed to follow a simple trend, and harmonize with website details such as images, text, and logos.
