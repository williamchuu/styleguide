LOGOS

The image of Ketbell and half an orange represents the harmonious combination of exercise and nutrition to maintain good health is the main symbol of our brand.


The preferred approach is to use only the icon, or the wordmark logo individually. We encourage everyone to use this logo to choose the colors we recommend below, and especially the background image when you place the logo should choose neutral colors. This will help to present the logo more flexibly for each page to create a consistent, modern, and neat look. Also, We apply the principle of design repetition of the inside of the orange. We want a logo to be more vivid, create consistency, and create visual interest.


The exclusion area around our logo should be left blank. In the app, the logo size should be maintained with a width of 68px, and a height of 96px (wordmark was created with Sansita font, 30px, bold) in order to align the logo to a perfect size. Usually half the width of the logo itself, these non-graphic areas give the logo space to breathe to ensure they maintain a visual impact.
