LOGOS

The logo is a hand holding a bitten apple. The bitten apple represents the food that goes to waste, and the hand represents the goal of giving away the food to someone else. The typeface used in the wordmark is PT Sans. This typeface uses modern humanist san serif shapes that make it aesthetically pleasing and easy to read.


The primary logo is the vertical version with or without the wordmark. The horizontal logo is used on webpages or environments where the vertical logo doesn’t fit.


The shapes have round corners so that they’re easier on the eyes. Warm colors are chosen to stimulate taste buds and the appetite. The shape of the apple is carefully constructed with circular eyes to ensure that the logo will be aesthetically pleasing to the human eye.


The area between the wordmark and logo mark should be the same size of the wordmark. This space ensures that the logo doesn’t appear tense.
