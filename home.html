OUR THEORY

When we develop design ideas for brands to respond to users to give them an impressive experience of our app. Here, we introduce a styleguide that builds and develops the core of the brand while keeping the customer experience through innovative designs.


We combine bright colors to create a dynamic, playful space, and build trust for customers about the app. We use kettlebell logos and orange interlocking often with a mix of Shadow Green and Rose Fog colors. And the typeface that we use is to create harmony and clarity with the difference in text size between the heading and the text, so the reader will interact more easily with the app.
