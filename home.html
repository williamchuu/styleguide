OUR THEORY

We are a brand that strives to minimize the amount of food that goes to waste.


Our aesthetic is to appear friendly, simple, and inviting: We use warm colors to create an energetic space for our users. Our typefaces are appealing and reader-friendly.


Thirty five million tonnes of food is lost or wasted. About one third of the food is still perfectly edible. With our app, users can safely donate their unused food or groceries to someone else who is willing to take it. We encourage our users to minimize the amount of food they throw out to make our planet more sustainable for the future.
