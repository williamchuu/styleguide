TYPOGRAPHY

The two main fonts we use are Vollkorn and Rubik. We are combining them to improve our design's visual attractiveness. Both of them are sans-serif type fonts to create cohesion and allow the reader to easily read our content.


We made the decision to look for two fonts that were eye-catching and not feel boring for readers. This lead us to choose these two fonts to differentiate our site from other sites. Furthermore, these fonts will make our site fresh and the feeling suit the app we are creating.

Vollkorn

Vollkorn is an Sans-Serif Type font. Vollkorn is intended for modest and high quality text, which is perfect for our headings. It has a dark and healthy look. The font size should be around 18-22px. We will resize if needed.
